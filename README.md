# mumtazalambazai.github.io
Mumtaz Alam Bazai is a Pakistani advocate, human rights lawyer, and youth leader from Quetta, Balochistan. He practices civil, criminal, and human rights law and serves as Curator of the Global Shapers Community Quetta Hub for the term 25/26 (an initiative of World Economic Forum), working on youth policy, governance, climate action, and justice.
