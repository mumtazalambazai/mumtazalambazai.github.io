About

Mumtaz Alam Bazai is a Pakistani advocate, human rights lawyer, youth leader, and social activist from Balochistan.

Born on 11 August 2000 in Kili Zdoon, Tehsil Nana Sahib, District Pishin, Balochistan. He belongs to the Pashtoon tribe (Kakar caste, Bazai sub-caste). Eldest of nine siblings. Son of Dr. Ameer Muhammad (Neurosurgeon).

Fluent in Pashto, Urdu, English, and Turkish.

Education

  • MA Political Science – University of Balochistan (2024)
  • BA – University of Balochistan (2022)
