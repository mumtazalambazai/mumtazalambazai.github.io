The Journey

Born on 11 August 2000 in Kili Zdoon, District Pishin, Balochistan. Mumtaz is the eldest of nine siblings, son of Dr. Ameer Muhammad (Neurosurgeon). From village schooling to scholarship-based excellence at Pak-Turk Maarif, his journey is defined by discipline and brilliance.

2019 - Intermediate

Pak-Turk Maarif International Schools & Colleges. Demonstrated leadership and multilingual fluency (Pashto, Urdu, English, Turkish).

2022 - BA (Arts)

University of Balochistan. Building the foundation for social sciences and political thought.

2024 - LLB (Hons) & MA Pol. Sci.

University Law College & University of Balochistan. Graduated with honors, mastering the intricacies of law and governance.

2025 - Legal License

Received Bar Council License (Reg No. 14/2025). Formally joined Iqra Law Associates under Advocate Abdul Zahir Kakar.
