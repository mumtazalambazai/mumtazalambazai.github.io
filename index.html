Journey & Early Life

Mumtaz Alam Bazai is a Pakistani advocate and human rights lawyer from Balochistan, committed to justice, constitutionalism, and youth empowerment. Born on 11 August 2000 in Kili Zdoon, District Pishin, his journey reflects resilience, scholarship, and leadership.

Education

  • LLB (Hons.) – University Law College (2024)
  • MA Political Science – University of Balochistan (2024)
  • BA – University of Balochistan (2022)
  • Pak-Turk Maarif International Schools (2019)

Legal Career

Licensed advocate (Bar Reg. No. 14/2025), trained at Iqra Law Associates, Quetta. Actively practicing civil, criminal, and human rights litigation with strong pro-bono engagement.

Leadership & Impact

Curator, Global Shapers Quetta Hub (WEF). Represented Pakistan at Global Shapers Annual Summit 2025, Geneva. Architect of Balochistan’s first Youth Policy initiative.
