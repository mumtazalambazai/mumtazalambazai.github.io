Mumtaz Alam Bazai

Advocate | Human Rights Lawyer | WEF Global Shaper

The Visionary

Mumtaz Alam Bazai is a beacon of justice in Balochistan. A High Court Advocate, Human Rights Defender, and Youth Leader committed to the Rule of Law.

Early Life & Roots

Born on 11 August 2000 in Kili Zdoon, Pishin. Son of Dr. Ameer Muhammad (Neurosurgeon). The eldest of nine, Mumtaz embodies the resilience of the Pashtun Kakar heritage. Fluent in Pashto, Urdu, English, and Turkish, his journey from village schooling to Pak-Turk Maarif International set the stage for global impact.

Academic Excellence

  • LLB (Hons.) — University Law College Quetta (2024)
  • MA Political Science — University of Balochistan (2024)
  • BA (Arts) — University of Balochistan (2022)
  • Intermediate — Pak-Turk Maarif International (2019)

Legal Dominion

Formally entering the legal arena in 2025 (Bar Council Reg. No. 14/2025), Mumtaz trained under the legendary Advocate Abdul Zahir Kakar at Iqra Law Associates.

He actively undertakes Pro Bono Litigation for the marginalized, fighting in Civil, Criminal, and Constitutional courts to uphold human dignity.
