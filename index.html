About Mumtaz Alam Bazai

Mumtaz Alam Bazai is a Pakistani advocate, human rights lawyer, youth leader, and social activist from Balochistan. He is committed to justice, rule of law, and youth empowerment, and is widely recognized for his work in civil, criminal, and human rights law alongside strong civic leadership.

Born on 11 August 2000 in Kili Zdoon, Tehsil Nana Sahib, District Pishin, Balochistan, Pakistan, he belongs to the Pashtoon tribe (Kakar caste, Bazai sub-caste). He is the eldest of nine siblings. His father, Dr. Ameer Muhammad, is a neurosurgeon, and his mother is a homemaker.

From early education in his village to scholarship-based schooling at Pak-Turk Maarif International Schools, Mumtaz consistently demonstrated academic excellence, leadership, discipline, and multilingual fluency in Pashto, Urdu, English, and Turkish.

Education

  • Intermediate – Pak-Turk Maarif International Schools & Colleges (2019)

Legal Career

Mumtaz formally entered the legal profession in 2025. He trained under Advocate Abdul Zahir Kakar at Iqra Law Associates, Quetta, gaining extensive experience in civil, criminal, and human rights litigation.

On 2 December 2025, he received his license to practice before the trial courts of Pakistan (Bar Council Reg. No. 14/2025). He actively undertakes pro bono litigation for marginalized and vulnerable communities.

Leadership & Social Impact

  • Curator – Global Shapers Community Quetta Hub (World Economic Forum)
  • Social Media Head – Global Shapers Community Quetta (2022–2024)
  • Cabinet Member – General Assembly Balochistan (2025)
  • Executive Member & Head of Legal Team – Human Rights Council of Balochistan
  • District Co-Head – Blood Heroes (2022–2024)
  • Volunteer – Millions Smiles, Bargad Organization, Alkhidmat Foundation

He represented Quetta Hub at the Global Shapers Annual Summit 2025 in Geneva, initiated Balochistan’s first Youth Policy Project, authored the Hub’s Governance Charter, spearheaded inter-hub collaborations, earned Best Speaker Award (2024), and served as an Honourable Delegate at Youth Model National Assembly 2025.

Publications & Research
